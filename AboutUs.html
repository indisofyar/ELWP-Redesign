About us

East London Waterworks Park is incorporated in England and Wales as a charitable company (Company Number 13154915). It is also a registered charity in England and Wales (Charity Number 1199414). The objective of the charity is to promote the conservation, preservation and improvement of the physical and natural environment for the public benefit by purchasing land, opening it up to public access and returning it to nature, and by promoting biodiversity and prudent use of natural resources.

Registered office: 45 Lyne Crescent, Walthamstow, London, E17 5HY

Email address: info @ elwp.org.uk
