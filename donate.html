Donate

  1. Home

As a group of local volunteers, we wouldn't be where we are today without all the friends, supporters and donors who continue to back us. By making a donation, you are contributing to phase two of the campaign, which will focus on fundraising to purchase the site. If East London Waterworks fails to purchase the land as planned, your donation will be used to support relevant, local projects in accordance with our charitable objects. If we raise more than enough to purchase the land, we will use any surplus generated to support the costs of transformation and operation of the Park for the community.

If you would like to make a donation directly to East London Waterworks Park, please fill in your details above and click the Donate button.

Your payment will be handled by Paypal. You do not need to be registered with Paypal to use the Paypal system. You will be given a choice between paying with a Payal account (if you have one) and paying by credit or debit card.

Any details that you supply to Paypal (such as credit card numbers) will be invisible to East London Waterworks Park. The only information that Paypal will transmit to East London Waterworks Park will be your name and confirmation that your payment has been made or notification that it has failed.
